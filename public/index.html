About Us

Friday Knights is a weekly fifth edition Dungeons and Dragons real play adventure. New episodes release every Monday so make sure to subscribe in your favorite podcast application.

Artio Secullus

A wood elf raised in seclusion with only animals for friends. Now the leader of the "Gwynvengers" as they attempt to save the world from elemental cultists.

Fellkyn Kyllgraive

An Eladrin with a sorted past who uses odd magic. Now tied to Gwyndol he travels around the world in search of old magics and secrets.

Myrloch

A determined Firbolg in search of the clan of orcs that destroyed his homelands. Now training as a monk, and traveling with new friends to help expidite the process.
