About Us

Friday Knights is a weekly fifth edition Dungeons and Dragons real play adventure. The current story is based on the Curse of Strahd module. New episodes release every Monday so make sure to subscribe in your favorite podcast application

AZRAEL JASNY-WILK

A young aasimar, unaware of his true heritage, born and raised in the Order of the Gauntlet.

EZIEKIEL “SUEE” MCGUFF

An energetic wood elf druid that was recruited by the Emerald Enclave. He has been raising pigs for the last hundred years of his life.

ZION EVERKNIGHT

A middle aged man with no memory of his past. He enjoys walks in the dark and shooting his gun.
